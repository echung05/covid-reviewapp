About "What's Changed?"

Like many places, the COVID-19 pandemic has had an immense impact on the local community of Charlottesville, VA. What's Changed was created to track and understand the changes that COVID-19 has inflicted onto businesses in the area. Serving as a one stop shop for understanding everything COVID-related to a business. This includes information regarding outdoor seating, COVID protocol ratings, maximum capacity, etc. In addition, users can submit their own reviews and commentary regarding certain businesses. Now more than ever, local communities must be connected with one another to support one another. Use What's Changed to do your part and help your local community.

What's Changed is a work programmed by five University of Virginia Students for a CS 3240 project. These students include Esther Chung, Diana Damenova, Brendan Pulju, David Tran, and Naqia Zaveri. The Django framework was used for the backend, Bootstrap 4 was used for front end work, Postgres was used for database services, Google's API was used for the map feature, Travis was used for integration testing, and Heroku was used for deployment.
